Infrastructure







 
Well established Training centre
 
For conducting in house training program and on the job training HRKVK has a well equipped training hall along with hostels having a capacity to accommodate 50 members.
 
FARMS
 
HR KVK has demonstration unit to educate the farmers of entire district which has well established various units such as Animal husbandry, Sericulture, Mushroom culture, Orchard, precision farming, Horticulture units-nursery production, poly houses etc. situated in an aerial extent of 50 acres. Each unit is performing its function well and educating proven solution to the farm related problems.  These units would be well utilized for on-the-job training to the farmers.
 

Soil test lab:
 

HRKVK has well established soil testing laboratory equipped with all the machines and equipments and testing the soil samples from the district regularly.
 
Equipments and Vehicles:
 

HRKVK has jeep, two wheelers, Tractor 2 nos., Trailor 2 nos., Cultivator 1 no., land leveler 1 no., cage wheel 1 no., rotovator 1 no., bund former 1 no., disc plough, cono weeder, rain gun, sprinkler 1 unit, sprayers (power, battery and rocker) and small implements.
 

Fully furnished office with computer, digital camera and audio visual lab.

 

Automatic Weather Station (AWS) 

 

Web linkage with Dynamic Market Information (DMI) of TNAU
