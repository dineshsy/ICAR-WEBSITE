10th Scientific Advisory Committee Meeting of ICAR KVK Perambalur




10th Scientific Advisory Committee meeting of ICAR-KVK Perambalur was held on 21st of January 2019. As per the guidelines of ATARI, Zone X, Members have been selected from various departments such as Agriculture, horticulture, Animal Husbandry, Agriculture Engineering Department, Mahalir Thittam, Forestry, LEAD bank manager, NABARD, IIFPT etc., for this meeting. Meeting was chaired by Dr.K.Varadharaajen, hairman, Roever KVK, Perambalur. Dr. P. Bhaskaran, Principal Scientist, Mr. Easwaran, Joint Director of Agriculture, Perambalur, Mr. Santhanakrishnan, Deputy Director, FTC, Perambalur, Dr.EstherSheela, Joint Director ofAnimal Husbandryi/c. Special address given by Chairman, Roever KVK, Perambalur followed by Dr. P. Bhaskaran, Principal Scientist, Zone X, Hyderabad. Dr. V. E. Nethaji Mariappan, Senior Scientist and Head have presented the Annual Review for the year 2017 -18 to January 2019 and also the previous year recommendations. Presentation went on well followed by a discussion on the recommendation and further action which was noted by our team members. Individual department officials necessitated the recommendation from the line departments when Project Director, Mahalir Thittam was interact in associate with us for EDP training. Joint Director of Animal Husbandry formally welcomes to join the training programme for the coming year. And also he applauded the work done of ICAR KVK, Perambalur and suggested recommendation for further action.
