How to Reach KVK?

From Trichy Central Bus stand any route buses going to Chennai, Villupuram and Vellur will take you to Perambalur.

(OR)

From Trichy Chatram bus stand any route buses going to Perambalur and Cuddalore will take you to Perambalur.

From there, you can get into town bus No.1 series and get down at Vallapuram bus stop.

HRKVK is 11 kms away from Perambalur town.

If you are coming by train, you have to get down at Ariyalur and outside of the railway station, you have to pick up Perambalur bus.
